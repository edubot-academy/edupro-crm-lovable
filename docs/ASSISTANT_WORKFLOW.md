# Assistant Workflow - Assistant Role

## Overview
This document describes the daily workflow for assistant staff in the EduPro CRM system. Assistant staff provide support to sales and management teams, handling administrative tasks and customer inquiries.

## Daily Tasks

### 1. Monitor Incoming Communications
**Screen:** Notifications Page (`/notifications`)

**Steps:**
- Check notifications for new messages and alerts
- Review system notifications about enrollment approvals
- Respond to customer inquiries promptly

**Actions:**
- Click on notifications to view details
- Navigate to related pages (deals, enrollments, etc.)
- Take appropriate action based on notification type

### 2. Support Sales Team
**Screen:** Leads Page (`/leads`) and Deals Page (`/deals`)

**Steps:**
- Assist sales staff with lead follow-up when needed
- Help update lead information
- Support deal management tasks as requested

**Actions:**
- View leads and deals assigned to sales team
- Update lead qualification status when instructed
- Add notes to leads/deals based on customer interactions

### 3. Handle Customer Inquiries
**Screen:** Contacts Page (`/contacts`)

**Steps:**
- Look up customer information when inquiries come in
- Check customer history (previous courses, payments, etc.)
- Provide information to customers about their status

**Actions:**
- Search for customer by name, phone, or email
- View contact details for complete information
- Check deal and payment history

### 4. Assist with Enrollment Process
**Screen:** Enrollments Page (`/enrollments`) - **Admin only**

**Steps:**
- Help with enrollment data entry (requires admin role access)
- Verify enrollment information is complete
- Support enrollment approval process (if authorized)

**Actions:**
- View pending enrollments (admin only)
- Check enrollment details for accuracy
- Note any issues that need manager attention

**Note:** Enrollment management is restricted to admin role. Assistants should escalate enrollment-related tasks to admin.

### 5. Track Trial Lessons
**Screen:** Trial Lessons Page (`/trial-lessons`)

**Steps:**
- Monitor scheduled trial lessons
- Check trial lesson attendance and outcomes
- Update trial lesson status after completion

**Actions:**
- View upcoming trial lessons
- Mark attendance when trial is complete
- Add notes about trial outcome

### 6. Manage Tasks
**Screen:** Tasks Page (`/tasks`)

**Steps:**
- Review assigned tasks
- Complete administrative tasks
- Create tasks for follow-up items

**Actions:**
- Mark tasks as complete when done
- Add notes about task completion
- Create new tasks for items that need tracking

## Key Screens

| Screen | URL | Purpose |
|--------|-----|---------|
| Notifications | `/notifications` | View system alerts and messages |
| Contacts | `/contacts` | View and manage customer information |
| Leads | `/leads` | Support sales with lead management |
| Deals | `/deals` | Support sales with deal management |
| Trial Lessons | `/trial-lessons` | Track trial lesson schedule |
| Tasks | `/tasks` | Manage assigned tasks |
| Enrollments | `/enrollments` | Assist with enrollment process (admin only) |

## Support Responsibilities

### Customer Support
- Answer general customer questions
- Provide information about course schedules
- Check payment status for customers
- Direct complex issues to sales or management

### Administrative Support
- Data entry and verification
- Document organization
- Schedule coordination
- Communication management

### Team Support
- Assist sales with follow-up tasks
- Support manager with administrative needs
- Help with report preparation
- Coordinate between departments

## Tips

- Always verify customer information before providing details
- Document all customer interactions with notes
- Escalate issues that require sales or management attention
- Keep task list updated for accountability
- Proactively communicate about potential issues
